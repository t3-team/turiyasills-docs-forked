# Steps on How to Share your Jobs Postings

#### (https://scribehow.com/shared/Steps_on_How_to_Share_your_Jobs_Postings__sOK0ZJCvTruKD3HHSKTu8g)

This guide provides a comprehensive walkthrough for updating company settings and effectively managing job postings on the Turiyaskills platform. It covers essential features like uploading resumes, importing candidates from external networks, and customizing job postings for better visibility. By following this guide, users can streamline their hiring process, enhance candidate engagement, and ensure a professional presentation of job opportunities. It's an invaluable resource for anyone looking to optimize their recruitment efforts.
