# Credit Management

Turiyaskills uses a credit-based system for assessments. Understanding how credits work is essential for efficiently managing your assessment resources.

## Credit System Overview

AI Credits are consumed when you:
- Create new assessments (surveys and quizzes using AI engine)
- Subjective answer evaluation in Quizzes
- Video Proctoring
- Generate dynamic AI reports
- Use JD parser, Automated Skill clustering and Assessment creator based on the JD
- Resume parser, score against JD

Response Credits are consumed when you:
- Receive assessment response (surveys and quizzes)

## Credit Packages

We offer flexible subscription plans to suit different needs:

- Starter: 200 response credits & 1000 AI credits per month
- Pro: 500 response credits & 5,000 AI credits per month
- Enterprise: Custom credit allocation
- On top of this Top-Up response and AI credits on need basis

## Credit Usage Best Practices

1. **Plan Assessments Carefully**
   - Review credit requirements before creating assessments
   - Choose appropriate assessment types based on requirements whether you need a Survey or a Quiz
   - Survey is chosen when you want to collect information, opinions, or feedback from respondents. It is designed to gather data and insights for analysis.
   - Quiz is to test knowledge, skills, or memory. Typically includes questions with definitive "right" and "wrong" answers

2. **Monitor Credit Balance**
   - Regular checking of credit usage
   - Track credit consumption patterns in the Billing Info>Usage History page

## Credit Expiry

- Credits expire by the end of the cycle. For example: for monthly subscription plans credits are accumulated every month but expired en masse by the the end of the year cycle 
- Unused credits are carried forward to the next month but expired by end of year cycle.

## Real-World Credit Usage Scenarios

### Scenario 1 – Company A: Resume Matching & JD Scoring

**Use Case:**
Company A is hiring a Software Engineer. They have 200 resumes from applicants and want to quickly identify the top 20 candidates who best match their Job Description (JD).

**How Turiyaskills Helps:**

1. **Upload JD → JD Parser & Skill Clustering**
   - 1 JD parsing = ~10 AI Credits
   - Skill clusters are automatically generated

2. **Upload Candidate Resumes → Resume Parser + Scoring against JD**
   - Each resume parsing + scoring = 2 AI Credits per resume
   - 200 resumes × 2 = 400 AI Credits

3. **AI-Generated Candidate Ranking Report**
   - Dynamic AI report = 20 AI Credits

**✅ Credit Usage Summary (Company A)**
- JD Parsing + Skill Clustering → 10 AI Credits
- Resume Parsing + JD Scoring (200 resumes) → 400 AI Credits
- AI Report → 20 AI Credits
- **Total = 430 AI Credits**

**Value:** Instead of manually screening 200 resumes, Company A uses ~430 AI Credits and gets a ranked shortlist of top-fit candidates within minutes.

### Scenario 2 – Company B: AI-Generated Assessment for Screening

**Use Case:**
Company B is hiring for a Data Analyst role. They want to filter candidates after resume screening by conducting a technical assessment.

**How Turiyaskills Helps:**

1. **Assessment Generation**
   - HR uploads the JD
   - Turiyaskills generates a 5-section connected assessment (Multiple Choice + Technical Questions + Case Study)
   - Each section = 5 AI Credits
   - 5 sections = 25 AI Credits

2. **Candidate Participation**
   - 50 shortlisted candidates are invited
   - Each candidate submission = 1 Response Credit
   - 50 candidates = 50 Response Credits

3. **Evaluation & Proctoring**
   - Objective Questions = auto-graded (no extra AI Credits)
   - Technical Questions (2 questions × 50 candidates = 100 evaluations)
   - Each technical answer evaluation = 2 AI Credits
   - 100 evaluations = 200 AI Credits
   - Video Proctoring (if enabled) = 10 AI Credits per candidate
   - 50 × 10 = 500 AI Credits

4. **Dynamic AI Report**
   - Assessment summary with candidate insights = 20 AI Credits

**✅ Credit Usage Summary (Company B)**
- Assessment Creation → 25 AI Credits
- Candidate Responses → 50 Response Credits
- Technical Evaluations → 200 AI Credits
- Video Proctoring (optional) → 500 AI Credits
- AI Report → 20 AI Credits
- **Total = 745 AI Credits + 50 Response Credits**

**Value:** Company B ensures only the top-performing candidates proceed to interviews, saving recruiter time and ensuring fair, AI-backed evaluation.

## FAQs

### What are AI Credits?
AI Credits are the units used to measure AI processing resources. They are consumed when using AI-powered features like survey generation, report creation, and video analysis.

### What are Response Credits?
Response Credits are consumed for each candidate submission in features like questionnaires, tests, or video interviews. One response credit is used per candidate submission.

### How are credits calculated?
Credits are calculated based on the specific feature and its complexity. For example, generating assessment questions costs 5 AI Credits per batch of 10 questions, while video proctoring uses 10 AI Credits per candidate response.

### How are Assessment Generation Credits Calculated?
Assessment Generation uses 5 AI Credits per batch of up to 10 questions. If you need more questions, additional batches will be used accordingly.

### How do Dynamic AI Reports consume credits?
Dynamic Reports use a combination of text generation (5 credits) and image generation (5 credits per image). Each report includes text and 3 images, totaling 20 credits per report.

### How does Video Proctoring credit usage work?
Video Proctoring consumes 10 AI Credits per candidate response for analyzing the video and generating the proctoring evaluation report.

### When are credits deducted?
Credits are deducted in real-time as features are used. AI Credits are consumed when generating content or analyzing data, while Response Credits are used when collecting candidate submissions.

### Can I get refund for unused credits?
Credits are non-refundable but they never expire, so you can use them at any time for any supported feature.

### How can I purchase more credits?
You can purchase additional credits through your account dashboard. Various packages are available to suit different usage needs.
