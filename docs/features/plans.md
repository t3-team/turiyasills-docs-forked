# Flexible Plans

Turiyaskills offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate various user needs, ranging from small teams to large enterprises requiring advanced features.

## Pricing Plans

### 📌 Starter Plan

🎯 **Target Audience:** Small teams, individual recruiters, early-stage startups

**Cost:** ₹1,800 /Per month incl. GST

🎁 **Special Offer:** First month FREE! Subscription charges start from the second month only.

#### What is included:
- ✅ Single-user license
- ✅ 2000 AI Credits + 200 Response Credits / month
- ✅ Top Up AI & Response Credits when you need
- ✅ Skill-based assessments & AI-powered evaluations
- ✅ Resume Parsing AI-Generated Job Description Insights
- ✅ AI-Powered Coding Evaluation & Skill Gap Analysis
- ✅ AI-Powered Video Proctoring
- ✅ Survey & Quiz Creation with Unlimited Questions
- ✅ AI-Powered Text Generation in Report Builder
- ✅ AI-Powered Image Generation in Report Builder
- ✅ Safe Exam Browser for secure, proctored assessments
- ✅ Google Docs Integration
- ✅ Unused Credits Carry Over (Expires at Year End)

🔹 **Best for:** Small businesses ready to streamline hiring and workforce upskilling with comprehensive AI features at a predictable monthly cost.

---

### 📌 Pro Plan

🎯 **Target Audience:** Growing startups, SMBs, multi-user recruitment teams

**Cost:** ₹9,999 /Per month incl. GST

🎁 **Special Offer:** First month FREE! Subscription charges start from the second month only.

#### What is included:
- ✅ Multi-user access (10 Users)
- ✅ 5,000 AI Credits + 500 Response Credits / month
- ✅ Top Up AI & Response Credits when you need
- ✅ All Starter Plan Features PLUS:
- ✅ On board your team (in Org domain)
- ✅ AI-Powered Text & Image Generation in Report Builder
- ✅ Advanced Resume Scoring Against Job Descriptions
- ✅ Job Posting Channel Analysis & Insights
- ✅ Bulk Upload of Questions via Excel & JSON
- ✅ Advanced Skill Gap Reports
- ✅ Third-Party Integrations (HRMS, ATS, etc.) as Add On
- ✅ Unused Credits Carry Over (Expires at Year End)

🔹 **Best for:** SMBs that need a scalable hiring + learning & development solution with automated AI-powered assessments.

---

### 📌 Enterprise Plan

🎯 **Target Audience:** Large enterprises, recruitment agencies, workforce analytics teams

**Cost:** Custom Pricing

#### What is included:
- ✅ Custom AI Credits & Response Credits Allocation
- ✅ Top Up AI & Response Credits when you need
- ✅ All Pro Plan Features PLUS:
- ✅ White-Label Solution as Add-On
- ✅ Advanced Analytics & AI-Powered Hiring Insights
- ✅ Third-Party Integrations (HRMS, ATS, etc.)
- ✅ API Access & Custom Assessment Pipelines
- ✅ Tailored Talent Intelligence & Workforce Skill Reports
- ✅ Dedicated Success Manager & Onboarding Support
- ✅ Unused Credits Carry Over (Expires at Year End)

🔹 **Best for:** Organizations that require custom workforce development, centralized hiring pipelines, and deep integrations.
