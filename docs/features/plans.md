# Flexible Plans

Turiyaskills offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate various user needs, ranging from individuals exploring the platform to enterprises requiring advanced features.

## Pricing Plans

### Free Plan

**Cost: ₹0** (No credit card required)

#### Includes:
- 100 AI Credits
- 20 Response Credits
- AI-Generated Surveys/Quizzes (5 AI Credits per generation)
- AI-Powered Q&A Explanations (2 AI Credits per explanation)
- AI-Powered Subjective Text Answer Evaluation (2 AI Credits per evaluation)
- Survey/Quiz Responses (1 Response Credit per user response)
- Unlimited Number of Questions per Survey/Quiz
- Google Docs Integration
- Safe Exam Browser
- Unused Credits Carry Over

*Ideal for users to try out the platform before committing to a paid plan.*

### Pro Plan

**Cost: ₹9,999** (Currently at 25% discount: ₹7,499 + GST with code TURIYA25)

#### Includes:
- All features in the Free Plan, plus:
- 1,000 AI Credits
- 500 Response Credits
- AI-Powered Text Generation in Report Builder (2 AI Credits per generation)
- AI-Powered Image Generation in Report Builder (5 AI Credits per generation)
- AI-Powered Text Generation in Dynamic Report Builder for Surveys (5 AI Credits per generation)
- AI-Powered Video Proctoring (10 AI Credits per response)
- AI-Powered Video Interview Screening (10 AI Credits per screening; releasing soon)
- Questions Upload via Excel & JSON

*Suitable for heavy users and small businesses requiring advanced functionalities.*

### Enterprise Plan

**Cost: Custom Pricing**

#### Includes:
- All features in the Pro Plan, plus:
- Custom AI Credits
- Custom Response Credits

*Designed for large organizations with geographically dispersed teams and complex requirements.*
