# Turiyaskills AI Interview Assessment Guide

![AI Interview Module](./AI-%20Interview%20Module.gif)

The **AI Interview** feature within the **SmartEval** section of the **Assessments** module allows you to conduct automated screenings where an **AI Avatar** interacts directly with candidates. This tool evaluates not just technical knowledge, but also behavioral traits and communication skills.

---

### Getting Started: Setting Up Your AI Interview

Navigate to **Assessments > SmartEval > AI Interview** to access the setup page.

#### 1. Define the Role & Scope

To ensure the AI asks relevant questions, you must categorize the position:

- **Category & Subcategory**: Select the broad industry and specific job function (e.g., Interdisciplinary Roles > Data Administration).
- **Specific Role**: Choose the exact job title to tailor the question bank.
- **Duration & Language**: Set the total length of the interview (e.g., 30 minutes) and the language in which it will be conducted.

#### 2. Choose the Interview Type

You can set up three distinct types of interview rounds based on your screening goals:

- **Warm Up - Introduction**: Focuses on the candidate's basic communication and relevancy to the role.
- **Role Related - Technical**: Evaluates domain-specific knowledge, technical articulation, and communication capabilities.
- **Behavioural - HR**: Assesses soft skills including problem-solving, teamwork, and overall communication.

#### 3. Preview Questions

Before finalizing, use the **Preview Questions** button to review the AI-generated queries (e.g., "How would you explain the term query in a database system?").

---

### How Candidates Experience the Interview

Once invited, candidates enter a dedicated AI Interview page.

- **Interaction**: The AI Avatar presents the **Main Question**.
- **Recording**: Candidates click the **Answer** button to record their response.
- **Controls**: Candidates have the option to **Repeat Question** if they need clarity or **Exit Interview** if necessary.

---

### Comprehensive Evaluation Criteria

The system automatically scores candidates across four key pillars:

| Evaluation Pillar | What is Analyzed |
| --- | --- |
| **Domain Knowledge** | **Response Accuracy** (relevancy to the question) and **Terminology Match Rate** (use of industry-standard key terms). |
| **Articulation & Problem Solving** | **Response Structure** (logical flow) and the use of **Support Examples** to back up claims. |
| **Teamwork & Leadership** | Behavioral positioning, specifically how the candidate functions as a **Team Player** and their **Leadership** potential. |
| **Communication & Body Language** | **Voice Pacing**, **Grammar**, and physical cues like **Eye Contact** maintained with the camera. |
