# How to Navigate the Features of Turiyaskills

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to effectively utilize the features of the Turiyaskills app, making it essential for users looking to streamline their recruitment process. It covers everything from job creation and resume parsing to team management and billing, ensuring users can maximize the app's capabilities. By following this guide, users can enhance their hiring efficiency and make informed decisions with AI-generated assessments and detailed analytics. It's a valuable resource for anyone aiming to optimize their recruitment strategy.
